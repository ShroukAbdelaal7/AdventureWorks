# AdventureWorks Sales & Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project delivers an interactive, end-to-end business intelligence solution designed to analyze global retail sales performance, order distributions, and operational KPIs. 

By extracting and transforming relational data using complex **SQL** scripts, the raw data was shaped into a star schema data model and visualized inside **Power BI** to provide actionable, executive-level insights.

![Sales Analytics Dashboard](assets\AdventureWorks_PBI.png)

---

## 💡 Key Insights & Features
* **Executive KPIs:** Real-time tracking of high-level health metrics including Total Orders (31K), Total Revenue ($110M SubTotal), Freight, and Taxes.
* **Temporal Trend Analysis:** Interactive line chart mapping order frequencies over a multi-year timeline to isolate seasonal spikes and structural shifts (e.g., the notable volume surge post-July 2024).
* **Geographical Distribution:** Dual-axis visualization combining volume columns and revenue lines to evaluate regional performance across global territories, highlighting Australia and the Southwest US as primary drivers.
* **Product Segmentation:** Dynamic Treemap segmenting order volume by major categories (Accessories, Clothing, Bikes, Components) paired with a market share breakdown by Group regions (North America, Europe, Pacific).

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* **Data Source:** AdventureWorks Relational Database
* **Data Extraction & Modeling:** SQL 
* **ETL & Data Transformation:** Power Query
* **Data Visualization & Analytics:** Power BI Desktop
